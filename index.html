About Haarlem

Haarlem is a city and municipality in the Netherlands. It is the capital of the province of North Holland and is situated at the northern edge of the Randstad, one of the most populated metropolitan areas in Europe. Haarlem had a population of 159,556 in 2017. Haarlem with its many historic buildings and beautiful old squares lies near the beach on the banks of the Spaarne river. It is wonderful to stroll through the narrow streets lined by the monumental buildings built by master Lieven de Key. Visit extraordinary museums, such as the Frans Hals or Teylers Museum, and explore its countless cafés and restaurants. Haarlem is called the most Flemish city of the north with reason! Haarlem is originally known for its flower growing district, Haarlem in the beginning was also known for its textiles, shipyards, engineering plants and textile mills.

About Amsterdamsewijk

The Amsterdamse Wijk is one of the older Haarlem neighborhoods.The district is also traditionally known as the Amsterdam neighborhood . Adjacent to the Amsterdamse Poort, this neighborhood dates back to the 14th century. It was not yet a residential area in Haarlem. The district served as protection for the city from the East.The district is located to the east of the center, directly under the Haarlem-Amsterdam railroad track. The neighborhood is originally a working-class neighborhood with densely built-up buildings and small houses built around 1900, particularly on the west side. To the east, the neighborhood borders on Parkwijk . There are more post-war buildings such as flats.Immediately south of the Haarlem-Amsterdam railway line is the Amsterdamsewijk, with nearly 8,000 residents. The western part in particular has a dense development. With small workers' houses from around 1900 and also younger houses as a product of urban renewal. The eastern part came into being later and also houses low flats just after the war. Amsterdamstraat is an important shopping area in the neighborhood. Amsterdamsestraat is the most important shopping area. Every Amsterdam neighborhood has its own character and charm, and its own shops, restaurants, cafés, museums and attractions. Take the time to discover the hidden gems in Amsterdam's neighborhoods.Amsterdamsewijk has a total of 660 business locations. These businnes locations can be grouped up in 7 different sectors: Lbv: Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries, Nen: Industry and Energy, Hh: Trade and Catering, Vic: Transport Information and Communication, Fdo: Financial Services and OnroerendGoed, Zd: Business services, Cro: Culture Recreation and Other Services.

HWA RANG-Jiu jitsu & Taekwondo

Taekwondo:

Taekwondo is originally a Korean martial art, where both hand and foot techniques are used. The literal translation of the word Taekwondo is: "The way of the foot and the fist". The word Taekwondo is made up of 3 parts:

  1. Tae (foot). The foot represents all leg techniques such as kicking and jumping.
  2. Kwon (fist). The hand stands for all hand and arm techniques such as bumps, blocks, gripping, clamping and throwing.
  3. Do (the way). The road is the path that you follow while practicing Taekwondo and mainly represents the mental part.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

The Brazilian Jiu Jitsu can be directly applied effectively and responds to the needs and perceptions of young people and adults through the personal approach. This new branch of sport focuses not only on physical defense but also on developing the right self-reflection.
